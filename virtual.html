About Our Montessori Program

Our Montessori program is designed to nurture the whole child - intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically. We follow the Montessori philosophy of education, which emphasizes hands-on, self-directed learning in a prepared environment.

  • Child-centered learning environment
  • Specially trained Montessori teachers
  • Development of independence and confidence
  • Focus on practical life skills
  • Individualized learning plans
  • Mixed-age classrooms promoting peer learning
